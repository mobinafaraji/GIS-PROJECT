Welcome to Our Air Quality WebGIS

Explore Air Quality in Italy

Explore interactive maps and analysis of air pollution across Italy.

Air pollution is a critical environmental concern, significantly impacting public health and the natural ecosystem, particularly in urban and industrial regions. In Italy, pollutants such as nitrogen dioxide (NO₂), particulate matter (PM₁₀ and PM₂.₅) frequently surpass the recommended safety limits, with concentrations notably high in the Po Valley and major metropolitan areas.These elevated pollutant levels are linked to a range of health issues, including respiratory diseases, cardiovascular conditions, and a decline in overall quality of life.

This WebGIS project provides a comprehensive spatial analysis of air quality across Italy, leveraging QGIS-based map styling, GeoServer publishing, and OpenLayers integration. The platform enables users to visualize and interact with air pollution data through dynamic maps, offering real-time pollutant concentration insights. It also incorporates interactive features such as dynamic legends and layer switchers, designed to facilitate data exploration. The goal of this project is to support informed decision-making, enhance public awareness, and contribute to effective environmental monitoring and policy formulation.
